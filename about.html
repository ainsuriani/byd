Who We Are

BYD (Build Your Dreams) is a global high-tech company leveraging innovation to improve lives. We focus on electronics, automotive, new energy, and rail transit.

Our Mission

We aim to lead in clean energy and transport, reduce fossil fuel dependence, and build a sustainable future.

Innovation & Sustainability

As a top manufacturer of EVs and batteries, BYD pioneers with tech like the Blade Battery and e-Platform 3.0 to drive eco-conscious innovation.

Global Impact

Operating in over 50 countries, BYD is shaping clean energy mobility around the world.
