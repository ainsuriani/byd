Who We Are

BYD (Build Your Dreams) is a leading global high-tech company devoted to leveraging technological innovations for a better life. We focus on four core industries: electronics, automotive, new energy, and rail transit.

Our Mission

Our mission is to lead the way in clean energy and transportation, reducing global dependence on fossil fuels and creating a more sustainable future.

Innovation & Sustainability

As one of the largest manufacturers of electric vehicles and rechargeable batteries, BYD is committed to innovation and sustainability. We have developed ground-breaking technologies to support our green vision, including the Blade Battery and e-Platform 3.0.

Global Impact

With a presence in over 50 countries and regions, BYD is shaping the future of mobility and energy worldwide.
