Cookies Policy

This Cookies Policy explains how BYD Malaysia uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small files stored on your device that help us analyze usage, remember preferences, and improve site performance.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential: Required for site functionality.
Analytics: Track interactions to optimize services.
Preferences: Store settings like language and theme.

3. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies via your browser. Disabling may affect functionality.
